Quiz Prompt Selection

Choose how the quiz prompt should be structured.

Example: "The apple pie has ___ oz of apples and ___ oranges." The answer choices are 3,4,6,7. This would be 4 answer choices.

The apple pie has ___ oz of apples.” would be 1 blank.

Examples

Amount Based

Question: A small fries is ___ oz and a large fries is ___ oz.

Answers: 4, 2, 6, 7

Correct Answer(s): 2

Type: Amount Based + Fill in the Blank

Ingredient Based

Question: The 2 ingredients in a burger are ___ and lettuce.

Answers: Apple, onion, nuggets, pineapple

Correct Answer(s): 1

Type: Ingredient Based + Fill in the Blank

True or False

Question: A burger contains onion and lettuce.

Correct Answer(s): 1

Type: Ingredient Based + True or False
